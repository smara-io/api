## Channels ranked by expected ROI

1. **Twitter replies** — free, direct access to target audience
2. **ClavHub** — free distribution, users discover organically
3. **Dev.to blog posts** — SEO, long tail traffic
4. **Reddit posts** — burst traffic, good for validation
5. **Framework integration PRs** — massive reach, takes effort
6. **Product Hunt** — one-time spike, good for credibility
7. **HN** — high risk/reward, can make or break
8. **AI directories** — passive discovery, low effort
9. **Discord communities** — warm audience, low volume

## What NOT to do yet
- Don't pay for ads (no budget, audience too small)
- Don't build a blog platform (use Dev.to)
- Don't create video content yet (time-intensive)
- Don't cold-email (bad reputation, low conversion)
- Don't over-automate posting (authenticity matters early)
